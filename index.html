IOTendance

IOTendance is an innovative solution to manual paper-based attendance marking systems, using an emerging technology – Bluetooth Beacons. Students have an app on their phone that searches for a beacon in range. If it detects a beacon, it automatically sends the physical attendance of that person to the web service which verifies their attendance digitally.
